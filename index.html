Cliffe, Sep.2021

masthead typography project


Shapes are found in everyday objects around us. Cliffe is a typographic exploration started by retrieving discarded boxes from the recycling bin. I flatten out the box nets and extracted the shapes created by the folds.

Released to UNHRC, Jun.2021

205x292mm, 16 pages

tracing paper, kraft paper


This publication is an exploration of images and text through three randomly assigned categories; earth, national and personal. By using the definition of national as a person belonging to a certain country, I related that to my own experience as a child of migrants, extended that to my mother’s own experience and even further to the collective experience of the Vietnamese refugee crisis.

Run Lola Run (again), Jun.2021

rotoscope animation


A redesign of a film opening title sequence exploring storytelling and motion graphic techniques. I chose Run Lola Run(1998), a German experimental thriller film, focusing on the idea of running in circles using objects from key moments in the film.
